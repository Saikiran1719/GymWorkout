Golden Rules
🏋️
Progressive Overload
Increase weight by 2.5–5 kg every 1–2 weeks. Never stagnate at the same load — constant stimulus = constant growth.
⏰
Workout Duration
Aim for 60–75 min per session. Over 90 min elevates cortisol — counterproductive for fat loss and muscle retention.
🔥
HIIT Protocol
20 sec sprint + 40 sec walk = 1 cycle. Do 10–12 cycles. Burns fat 3× faster than steady-state cardio.
🥩
Post-Workout Protein Window
Eat protein within 30 min of finishing — whey shake or eggs. Maximizes MPS (muscle protein synthesis) for recovery.
📊
Weigh In Correctly
Once a week, same day, same time (morning, after toilet). Daily fluctuations are water weight — track trends, not single readings.
🌙
Sleep is Non-Negotiable
80% of muscle repair and fat-burning hormones (GH, testosterone) release during deep sleep. Poor sleep = zero progress.
